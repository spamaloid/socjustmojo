About sjMojo


sjMojo was started by a group of dedicated volunteers who were looking for a simple way to find organizations that:

  • Did the kind of work we believed in
  • Helped people live better lives
  • Offered a variety of ways to get involved

In other words, we wanted a resource that compiled information on social-justice organizations.

Since we didn't find the exact resource we were looking for, we decided to build it ourselves!

We're always happy to entertain suggestions for additional content and organizations. And we like to answer questions, when we can. So feel free to reach out!
