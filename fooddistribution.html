Distribution of food


Thousands of people die from starvation in some places, whereas thousands of others suffer from obesity-related problems. The complicated logistics of food distribution have led to increasing numbers of both groups.

Transportation, supply-chain distrubiton, education and awareness, agricultural practices, and logistics are all challenges we must solve together.
